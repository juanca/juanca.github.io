🔧 Mac Mode Setup

  1. Download and install Conjure Bridge on your Mac
  2. Launch it — a PIN appears in the menu bar
  3. Click the menu bar icon → Share CA Certificate → AirDrop to Vision Pro
  4. On Vision Pro: Settings → General → VPN & Device Management → Install
  5. Settings → General → About → Certificate Trust Settings → Enable
  6. Open Conjure on Vision Pro, switch to Mac mode, enter the PIN

Steps 3-5 are one-time only. After that, just enter the PIN each session.
