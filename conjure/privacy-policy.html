Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

Conjure is a voice-controlled AI agent app for Apple Vision Pro. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data Conjure accesses, how it is used, and what is shared with third parties.

Data We Collect

Conjure does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us. We do not operate servers, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

Voice and Speech Data

Conjure uses Apple's Speech framework to transcribe your voice into text. Speech processing is handled by Apple's services according to Apple's privacy policy. Conjure does not record, store, or transmit audio data beyond the active transcription session.

Anthropic API (Cloud Mode)

When using Cloud mode, your text prompts and conversation history are sent directly from your device to Anthropic's API servers. This communication is between your device and Anthropic. Conjure does not intermediate, log, or have access to this traffic. Your use of the Anthropic API is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy and Anthropic's Terms of Service.

Your Anthropic API key is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us.

Local Network (Mac Mode)

When using Mac mode, Conjure connects to a bridge server running on your Mac over your local network using Bonjour/mDNS discovery. All communication stays on your local network between your Vision Pro and your Mac. No data is sent to external servers by Conjure in this mode.

Third-Party Services

Conjure does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

All app data, preferences, and API keys are stored locally on your device. Conjure does not use cloud storage or remote databases.

Children's Privacy

Conjure is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
